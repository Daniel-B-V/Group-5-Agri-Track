Farm Management

Our innovative platform helps farmers and agricultural businesses efficiently track their crops from harvest to consumption. Monitor yields, record usage, analyze trends, and optimize your farm's productivity with our easy-to-use digital tools. Say goodbye to paper records and embrace data-driven farming decisions.

Our Mission

Harvest Management

We created Agri-Track to help farmers efficiently manage and track their harvests, ensuring optimal yields and reducing waste through smart planning tools.

Food Waste Reduction

Our platform connects farmers with donation networks to redistribute excess crops, solving food waste while helping communities in need.

Farm Management

Comprehensive tools to manage all aspects of your farm operations, from planting schedules to inventory tracking and sales management.

Our Story

Agri-Track was founded in 2025 by a team of tech enthusiasts who saw the need for better farm management solutions. We noticed that:

  • 30-40% of food produced is wasted before reaching consumers
  • Farmers struggle with inefficient harvest tracking methods
  • There's no easy way to connect excess produce with those in need

Our solution combines cutting-edge technology with simple, farmer-friendly interfaces to solve these problems.
